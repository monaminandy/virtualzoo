SET UP PROCESS

STEP 1: Open the VR glasses headset.

STEP 2: Open the video in your smartphone.

STEP 3: Place your smartphone in the holding tray.

STEP 4: Align the screen in the center.

STEP 5: Close the VR headset.

STEP 6: Place the VR headset on your head.

STEP 7: Adjust the spacing of the lenses.

STEP 8: Adjust the focus.

STEP 9: Adjust the sound of the video.

STEP 10: Use your head movements to control the cursor.

And that's it, you are READY TO GO
